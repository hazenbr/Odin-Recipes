Hamburgers

Description

Make these burgers yo. they mad good and settle in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz ground beef 20% fat
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • sea salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine ground beef and worcestershire sauce, form the burgers into 3.5 oz patties and season both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat cast iron pan over medium high heat. Place burger patties into heated pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, turning once.
  3. Remove from the pan and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
